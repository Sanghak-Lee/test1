Introduction

Since its establishment in 1992, Hanbo Chemicals has focused in the fields of importing, exporting and distributing chemicals and industrial products.
It has become the top brand name among the most reliable and professional chemical trading companies. We pride ourselves as a solid and sophisticated company.
Our considerable leap is credited to supplying high quality products and providing efficient assistance to our customers.
Hanbo Chemicals is also making preparations to join in venture with technically intensive industries in order to continue to expand our sphere of services and strive for excellence.
